Sword & Shield

#1 best weapon in the game! Excels at both offense and defense, very mobile, and you can heal while sheathed!

  • Originated in Monster Hunter (PS2)
  • Difficulty Rating: 1/5
  • Balanced in either physical or elemental damage. Also effective for ailments!

Switch Axe

A hard hitting weapon that has a bit of a learning curve, but once mastered can deliver fluid and exciting gameplay.

  • Originated in Monster Hunter Tri (Wii)
  • Difficulty Rating: 3/5
  • Balanced in either physical or elemental damage.

Hunting Horn

A underrated weapon personally, and serves as an aggresive blunt weapon that also buffs the party. And you can breakdance!

  • Originated in Monster Hunter 2 (PS2)
  • Difficulty Rating: 2/5
  • Focused more for physical damage. Buffs include health, offense, defense, and more!

Great Sword

Ole reliable, the most damaging weapon in the game but trades for poor mobility. Still very fun!

  • Originated in Monster Hunter (PS2)
  • Difficulty Rating: 4/5
  • Exceptionally powerful physical damage.
Charge Blade

An incredibly popular weapon and for good reason. A great offensive and defensive weapon, but is the hardest to master of all weapon types.

  • Originated in Monster Hunter 4 (3DS)
  • Difficulty Rating: 5/5
  • Balanced in either physical or elemental damage.
Long Sword

Known to be the most used weapon by Hunters, earned by it's good DPS, great mobility, and very flashy moves.

  • Originated in Monster Hunter 2 (PS2)
  • Difficulty Rating: 2/5
  • Focused more for physical damage, though capable of powerful elemental damage.
